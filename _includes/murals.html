The Murals — Four AR Experiences

Point your camera. Watch the wall open up.

○ 01 ×

American Flag

An animated flag waves against a sky of fireworks while a sparkler burns from the viewer's own hand. Patriotic audio. Fourth of July energy.

○ 02 ×

Zest Bee

A bee drifts from the wall, hovers close, follows the viewer around the corner. It buzzes in your pocket. It has opinions about the weather.

○ 03 ×

My Heart Belongs to Bartow

Flowers bloom and sway. A butterfly crosses the frame. 3D text hangs in the air over the mural. Ambient music. A love letter to the city.

○ 04 ×

Panther

A panther leaps from inside the wall — rendered with a depth mask so it reads as emerging from behind the mural, not floating above it. It prowls the parking lot. It watches you back.
