L'appel du vide / The call of the void

Bartow Unframed is a location-based augmented reality experience that threads together four public murals across Bartow, Florida. Each mural becomes a doorway — to animated art, to ambient sound, to the city itself. A collaboration between USF Access 3D Lab, the City of Bartow Community Redevelopment Agency, and Augmented Info Systems.
