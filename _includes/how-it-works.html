How It Works

Four steps. No headset required.

  1. 01

    Download the app

    Available on iOS and Android. No account. No friction.

  2. 02

    Open the map

    An OpenStreetMap-powered view of Bartow surfaces all four mural locations alongside local businesses and points of interest.

  3. 03

    Walk to a mural

    Follow the map. Stand in front of the wall. Raise your phone.

  4. 04

    Watch it come alive

    The mural animates, speaks, moves. Tour the rest of the city in between sites.
